NOT SUPPORTED by data. All industries showed positive or neutral revenue growth while increasing diversity 2020-2024. High-diversity industries outperformed low-diversity industries by 81% in revenue growth.

Regarding M (Meaningful DEI Language & Blinding):

SUGGESTED by patterns. Disability inclusion shows most consistent implementation across industries (smallest variance), suggesting standardized "blinding" practices may be more effective for disability versus gender/race where cultural/industry factors create larger variation.

ACHIEVABLE based on data. Disability representation growing fastest (+7.2%/year) while remaining below representational parity, suggesting targeted growth without "over-hiring." Organizations successfully balance multiple diversity dimensions simultaneously.
